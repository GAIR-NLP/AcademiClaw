# Target Dancer Capability Description

## Dancer Basic Information
- **Dance style**: Locking (pure Locking style)
- **Experience level**: 2-5 years of systematic training with solid fundamentals
- **Technical mastery**: Has mastered the 8 core Locking elements
- **Current status**: Seeking technical breakthroughs and artistic advancement

## Mastered Locking Elements

### Core Element Proficiency
1. **Lock (freeze point)**
   - Basic lock: proficiently mastered
   - Variants: Knee lock, Chest lock, Double lock
   - Characteristics: Clear freeze points, good muscle control

2. **Point (pointing)**
   - Directions: 8 basic directions accurate
   - Coordination: Good body turn coordination
   - Rhythm: Accurate on downbeats

3. **Wrist roll**
   - Basics: Smooth wrist rotation
   - Rhythm: Stable at constant speed
   - Coordination: Stable arm positioning

4. **Scooby Doo**
   - Footwork: Standard footwork accurate
   - Center of gravity: Good transfer control
   - Movement: Clear direction changes

5. **Up lock**
   - Technique: Accurate upward lock
   - Space: Good upward extension
   - Transitions: Mostly smooth transitions to other locks

6. **Which-a-way (direction changes)**
   - Directions: Basic direction switches mastered
   - Center of gravity: Mostly stable during switches
   - Speed: Accurate at normal speeds

7. **Stop & Go**
   - Rhythm: Basic stop-go rhythm mastered
   - Control: Mostly accurate stop timing
   - Variation: Basic variations in the go sections

8. **Funky guitar**
   - Mimicry: Guitar-playing motions mostly accurate
   - Performance: Basic role-play sense
   - Musicality: Basic musical sense

## Dance Abilities Needing Improvement

### 1. Complex Rhythm Processing
- **Syncopation handling**: Timing of movements on "&" beats needs more precision
- **Multi-layered rhythm**: Ability to simultaneously handle different instrument rhythm layers needs improvement
- **Rhythm memory**: Quick mastery of complex rhythm patterns needs strengthening

### 2. Technical Precision and Speed Balance
- **Fast transitions**: Lock clarity decreases above 115 BPM
- **Muscle control**: Muscle control during fast movements needs to be more precise
- **Energy distribution**: Energy distribution in fast sequences needs optimization

### 3. Musical Expression and Performance Layers
- **Musical correspondence**: Movement-to-musical-detail correspondence needs to be tighter
- **Emotional expression**: Ability to express musical emotions through dance needs improvement
- **Performance layers**: Energy variation and focus management need to be richer

### 4. Creative Combination Ability
- **Element combinations**: Ability to create novel combinations of basic elements needs development
- **Improvisation**: Musical improvisation and creative response ability needs improvement
- **Style innovation**: Innovation within the traditional framework needs strengthening

## Expected Dance Improvements

### Technical Level Improvements
1. **Rhythm complexity**: Ability to handle complex rhythms including syncopation
2. **Technical precision**: Maintaining lock clarity and accuracy at 115 BPM
3. **Movement fluency**: More natural and smooth transitions between elements

### Artistic Level Improvements
1. **Musicality**: Deep integration of movement design with musical features
2. **Expressiveness**: Significant improvement in dance expressiveness and impact
3. **Creativity**: Ability to create novel and unique element combinations

### Comprehensive Ability Improvements
1. **Musical understanding**: Deeper understanding of musical structure and emotion
2. **Body control**: More precise control of all body parts
3. **Dance thinking**: Broader choreography and creative thinking

## Appropriate Challenge Level
- **Music tempo**: 115 BPM (moderately fast, challenging)
- **Rhythm complexity**: Includes syncopation and multi-layered rhythm
- **Technical difficulty**: Requires precise locks and complex element combinations
- **Artistic requirements**: Requires musical expression and performance layers

## Key Points
- Focus is on dance choreography ability, not teaching ability
- Creativity and innovation are encouraged, not limited to traditional combinations
- Emphasis on the quality of musical treatment and technical presentation
- Format must strictly follow output_format.md requirements
