# Dance Choreography Task Requirements

## Task Overview
Design an 8x8 counts Locking dance choreography to accompany the track "Funky Master Lock." The focus is on dance choreography ability, particularly musical treatment, technical presentation, and creative expression.

## Core Requirements

### 1. Musical Correspondence Depth
- **Rhythm treatment**: Movements must precisely correspond to the musical rhythm, including on-beats, half-beats, and syncopation
- **Instrument correspondence**: Movement design should consider corresponding to different instruments' characteristics
- **Structural correspondence**: Different musical sections should have corresponding movement characteristics
- **Emotional expression**: Express the music's emotions and energy changes through dance

### 2. Locking Technical Presentation
- **Element usage**: Use multiple Locking elements to demonstrate technical diversity
- **Technical quality**: Movements should be clear and accurate, conforming to Locking technical standards
- **Style maintenance**: Maintain the essence of Locking style, avoid style mixing
- **Innovative application**: Exercise creativity within the traditional framework

### 3. Creative Choreography Level
- **Element combinations**: Create novel and unique element combinations
- **Rhythm innovation**: Develop creative rhythm treatment approaches
- **Spatial usage**: Effectively use space to create visual layers
- **Performance expression**: Rich performance layers and emotional expression

## Choreography Structure

### Overall Structure
- **Total length**: 8 x 8 counts (64 beats)
- **Corresponding music**: "Funky Master Lock" (115 BPM Funk)
- **Section division**:
  - Intro: 1x8 counts (introduction)
  - Verse 1: 2x8 counts (verse 1)
  - Chorus 1: 2x8 counts (chorus 1)
  - Bridge: 1x8 counts (bridge)
  - Chorus 2: 2x8 counts (chorus 2)
  - Outro: 1x8 counts (ending)

### Section Focus
1. **Intro (beats 1-8)**: Establish groove, introduce dance atmosphere
2. **Verse 1 (beats 9-24)**: Foundational element showcase, establish technical credibility
3. **Chorus 1 (beats 25-40)**: Energy increase, lock sequences and rhythmic complexity
4. **Bridge (beats 41-48)**: Transition treatment, prepare for final climax
5. **Chorus 2 (beats 49-64)**: Performance climax, maximum energy and creative display
6. **Outro (beats 65-72)**: Controlled finish, complete the experience

## Technical Requirements

### Required Technical Elements
1. **Locking element diversity**: Use at least 5 different core Locking elements
2. **Syncopation treatment**: Include at least 2 explicit syncopation treatments
3. **Rhythm layers**: Demonstrate the ability to handle multiple rhythm layers
4. **Musical correspondence**: Movement design deeply corresponds to musical features
5. **Performance elements**: Include energy changes, spatial usage, focus management, and other performance elements

### Encouraged Creative Directions
1. **Element innovation**: Novel applications of traditional elements
2. **Combination creativity**: Unconventional element combinations
3. **Rhythm creativity**: Unique rhythm treatment approaches
4. **Performance creativity**: Novel performance expression approaches

## Evaluation Focus

### Primary Evaluation Dimensions (90%)
1. **Musical treatment ability** (40%)
   - Precision of movement-to-music correspondence
   - Complexity and accuracy of rhythm treatment
   - Ability to express musical emotions

2. **Technical presentation quality** (30%)
   - Accuracy and clarity of Locking technique
   - Fluency and control of movements
   - Diversity and appropriateness of element usage

3. **Creative choreography level** (30%)
   - Innovative use and combination of elements
   - Unique rhythm treatment approaches
   - Novel performance expression approaches

### Secondary Evaluation Dimensions (10%)
4. **Format compliance** (10%)
   - Whether it meets output format requirements
   - Whether the structure is clear and complete
   - Whether the language is professional and accurate
